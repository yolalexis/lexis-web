In the vocabulary contest, participants attempt to master as many words as possible in 21 days. The outcome is determined by the changes in the number of words mastered before and after the 21-day period.

Registration for every monthly contest closes on the 9th or 10th and opens on the 10th or 11th of the previous month. This means participants can start anytime at their discretion and the 21-day period starts from the day they join. At the close of each month, the top three contestants are announced. Users can tap to view details showing their own progress as well as the top three performers’ progress/results

To sign up for the contest, participants simply fill in a form and submit.

The prizes include free offline/online English courses as well as other gifts.
